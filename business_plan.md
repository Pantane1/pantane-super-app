# Pantane Super App Business Plan

## Executive Summary
Pantane is a unified app for African entrepreneurs, integrating business management, payments, marketplace, and learning. Target: Kenyan market, scalable to Africa. Revenue via freemium, fees, and partnerships.

## Market Analysis
- **Target Audience**: Small hustlers to enterprises in Kenya/Africa (est. 50M entrepreneurs).
- **Competitors**: M-Pesa, Flutterwave, local apps.
- **Opportunity**: Fragmented tools; Pantane offers one-stop solution.

## Product Features
- See README.md for details.

## Marketing Strategy
- Launch in Kenya with influencers and events.
- Partnerships: M-Pesa, local banks.
- Digital ads targeting entrepreneurs.

## Financial Projections
- Year 1: 100K users, $500K revenue (fees + subscriptions).
- Costs: $200K dev, $100K marketing.
- Break-even: 6 months.

## Risks & Mitigation
- Adoption: User education campaigns.
- Security: Encryption and compliance.

## Roadmap
- Q1: MVP (Biz + Pay).
- Q2: Add Market + Learn.
- Q3: AI and offline features.
